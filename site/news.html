There is a new SocNetV version coming up. It is based on Qt5 toolkit and it will be the first ever 1.x version of the project! SocNetV version 0.90 was the final Qt4 version. From now on, development will continue only in 1.x series and Qt5. So, if you want to follow SocNetV in the future, it's time to install Qt5 on your computer!
